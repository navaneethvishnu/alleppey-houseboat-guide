# Alleppey Houseboat Booking Guide

Alleppey (Alappuzha) is one of the most beautiful destinations in Kerala, famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises. This guide helps you choose the best Alleppey houseboat for your trip.

## Why Choose Alleppey Houseboat

A houseboat cruise in Alleppey offers:

- Peaceful backwater experience
- Traditional Kerala food
- Village life views
- Sunset and sunrise cruise
- Private and luxury houseboats
- Perfect for honeymoon and family trips

## Types of Houseboats in Alleppey

### Luxury Houseboats
Luxury houseboats include premium interiors, air conditioning, and modern amenities.

### Premium Houseboats
Premium houseboats offer comfortable stay with good facilities at reasonable prices.

### Budget Houseboats
Budget houseboats are ideal for affordable backwater trips.

## Alleppey Houseboat Price Guide
